# Constitution

## What This Is

dbl-paper is the academic paper formalizing Deterministic Boundary Layers.

## Governing Principles

1. **Claims are explicit and falsifiable.**
2. **Proofs are under stated axioms.** The axioms are constructed such that claims follow by construction.
3. **Non-goals are declared.** No overclaiming.

## What This Is Not

- Not an implementation.
- Not a tutorial.
- Not a policy recommendation.
